BENEFITS OF SOLAR PUMPS

Quick Payback

When offsetting diesel use, solar water pumps offer faster payback compared to other applications of solar energy.

Less Maintenance Cost

A properly installed solar pump has negligible maintenance costs. Over the 30 year average life of the system, there are minimal replacement costs.

Eco Friendly

By counterbalancing dependence on diesel, solar pumps significantly contribute in reducing the carbon footprint.
